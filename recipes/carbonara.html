Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is made with guanciale (cured pork), eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, spaghetti pasta, and lots of black pepper.

Italians don't add extra ingredients like cream, milk, garlic, or onions. Try this recipe if you want to make an authentic.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 pound guanciale
  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 3 large eggs
  • 10 tbsp pecorino (or parmesan)
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the guanciale in olive oil until browned an crispy.then drain on paper towels

  2. Boil the spaghetti in salted water. Drain and return to the pot. Let cool.

  3. Whisk the eggs, 1/2 of the cheese and some pepper in a bowl until smooth.

  4. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring quickly, until creamy.

  5. Stir in the guanciale, then top with the remaining cheese and more more black pepper.

  6. Enjoy!
